🏅 Rally.fun Scoring System - Quick Reference

4 Gates (0-2 each)

  • • Content Alignment
  • • Information Accuracy
  • • Campaign Compliance
  • • Originality & Authenticity

2 Quality Metrics (0-5)

  • • Engagement Potential
  • • Technical Quality

5 Engagement Metrics

  • • Retweets (log-scaled)
  • • Likes (log-scaled)
  • • Replies (log-scaled)
  • • Quality of Replies
  • • Followers of Repliers

Formula: Campaign Points = M_gate × Quality_Score × 100
Gate Multiplier: M_gate = 1 + 0.5 × (g_star - 1), where g_star = average of 4 gates
