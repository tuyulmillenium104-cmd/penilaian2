1.2 GATE MULTIPLIER FORMULA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

gate_pass = min(G1, G2, G3, G4) > 0  (SEMUA gate harus > 0)
g_star = avg(G1, G2, G3, G4)         (Rata-rata gate score)
M_gate = 1 + β × (g_star - 1)        (β = 0.5, Multiplier 0.5x - 1.5x)

| Gate Scores | g_star | M_gate | V6.0 Requirement |
|-------------|--------|--------|------------------|
| All 2/2 | 2.0 | 1.5x | **MANDATORY** |
| All 1.5/2 | 1.5 | 1.25x | NOT ACCEPTABLE |
| All 1/2 | 1.0 | 1.0x | NOT ACCEPTABLE |
| Any 0/2 | FAIL | N/A | DISQUALIFIED |

★ V6.0 RULE: ALL gates MUST be 2/2. Period. No exceptions.

2.7 VIRAL COEFFICIENT CALCULATION (VCC)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Menghitung k-factor (viral spread potential).

**VCC FORMULA:**
k = (Share Rate) × (Average Secondary Reach)

VCC COMPONENTS:
| Component | Calculation | V6.0 Target |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Primary Reach | Your followers + immediate reach | Baseline |
| Share Rate | % who share | > 1% |
| Secondary Reach | Average followers of sharers | > 10K |
| k-factor | Share Rate × Secondary Reach | > 1.0 |

**VIRAL COEFFICIENT TARGETS:**
| k-factor | Meaning | V6.0 Requirement |
|----------|---------|------------------|
| > 1.0 | VIRAL (exponential growth) | TARGET |
| 0.5-1.0 | NEAR-VIRAL (linear growth) | ACCEPTABLE |
| < 0.5 | NOT VIRAL (plateau) | REVISE |

★ V6.0 RULE: k-factor prediction MUST be ≥ 0.5

2.9 COMPETITIVE GAP EXPLOITATION 2.0 (CGE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tidak hanya mengalahkan top performer, tapi menemukan apa yang MEREKA SEMUA LEWATKAN.

**CGE PROCESS:**

1. ANALYZE TOP 10 PERFORMERS
   | Aspect | Analyze For |
   |--------|-------------|
   | Emotional Angle | What emotions did they use? |
   | Format | Thread vs Single? Length? |
   | CTA Type | What reply bait did they use? |
   | Viral Mechanics | What triggers did they include? |
   | Influencer Strategy | Did they target high-FR replies? |

2. IDENTIFY UNIVERSAL GAPS
   What did NONE of them do?
   | Gap Type | Example |
   |----------|---------|
   | Missing Emotion | No one used controversy |
   | Missing Format | No one used challenge CTA |
   | Missing Strategy | No influencer targeting |
   | Missing Angle | No contrarian take |

3. EXPLOIT THE GAP
   | Your Advantage | How to Exploit |
   |----------------|----------------|
   | First to [X] | Be the ONLY one with this angle |
   | Better execution of [Y] | Improve on existing approach |
   | Combination they missed | Combine multiple winning elements |

**CGE SCORE:**
| Score | Description | V6.0 Requirement |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| Gap Found + Exploited | Clear competitive advantage | TARGET |
| Gap Found | Know what to do | MINIMUM |
| No Gap Analysis | Flying blind | REVISE |

★ V6.0 RULE: MUST identify at least ONE competitive gap
