================================================================================
                    ★ FORMULA KALKULASI LENGKAP
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: GATE PASS & MULTIPLIER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

gate_pass = min(G1, G2, G3, G4) > 0  (SEMUA gate harus > 0)
g_star = avg(G1, G2, G3, G4)         (Rata-rata gate score)
M_gate = 1 + 0.5 × (g_star - 1)      (Multiplier 0.5x - 1.5x)

GATE MULTIPLIER TABLE:
| Gate Scores | g_star | M_gate | Effect |
|-------------|--------|--------|--------|
| All 2/2 | 2.0 | 1.5x | MAXIMUM BONUS |
| All 1.5/2 | 1.5 | 1.25x | GOOD BONUS |
| All 1/2 | 1.0 | 1.0x | BASELINE |
| Mixed 1-2 | ~1.5 | ~1.25x | PARTIAL |
| Any 0/2 | FAIL | N/A | DISQUALIFIED |

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: CAMPAIGN POINTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Campaign_Points = M_gate × Σ(W[i] × normalized_metrics[i])

Dimana:
- W[i] = weight untuk setiap metrik (0-1, campaign-specific!)
- normalized_metrics = [EP/5, TQ/5, log(RT+1)/max, log(LK+1)/max, 
                       log(RP+1)/max, QR, log(FR+1)/max]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: FINAL DISTRIBUTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

S_user = max(user_Q, 0)^α
share_user = S_user / Σ(S_all_users)
rewards_user = share_user × total_rewards

DISTRIBUTION CURVES (α):
| Curve | α | Top 10% Gets | Strategy |
|-------|---|--------------|----------|
| Balanced | 1.0 | ~25% | Broad competition |
| Default | 3.0 | ~90% | Must be TOP |
| Extreme | 8.0 | ~99% | Winner takes almost all |

                    ★ FR (FOLLOWERS OF REPLIERS) CHEAT CODE
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KENAPA FR SANGAT POWERFUL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FR Formula: log(Sum of Followers of All Repliers + 1)

CONTOH KALKULASI:
| Scenario | Followers | FR Score | Equivalent |
|----------|-----------|----------|------------|
| 1 reply dari akun 100K | 100,000 | log(100001) ≈ 5.0 | 100,000 likes |
| 10 replies dari akun 10K each | 100,000 | log(100001) ≈ 5.0 | 100,000 RTs |
| 1 reply dari akun 1M | 1,000,000 | log(1000001) ≈ 6.0 | 1,000,000 likes |
| 100 replies dari akun 100 each | 10,000 | log(10001) ≈ 4.0 | 10,000 likes |

★ INSIGHT: 1 reply dari influencer > 100 replies dari akun kecil!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FR OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STRATEGY 1: HIGH-FOLLOWER REPLY BAIT
Target: Dapatkan reply dari akun besar

Templates:
- "@[influencer] thoughts on this?" (direct mention)
- "Building on @[influencer]'s point about [X]..." (quote add)
- "I disagree with @[influencer] on [X] because..." (respectful challenge)
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian view on their topic]"

STRATEGY 2: THREAD JACKING
Target: Reply ke thread influencers untuk visibility

- Cari thread viral dari influencers
- Reply dengan value-adding comment
- Jika mereka reply back → FR BOOST!

STRATEGY 3: CONTROVERSY = HIGH-FOLLOWER REPLIES
Target: Bikin konten yang controversial untuk reply dari banyak orang

- "Unpopular opinion: [X]"
- "Change my mind: [Y]"
- Hot takes yang relevan dengan niche

STRATEGY 4: COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT
Target: Membangun hubungan dengan akun medium (10K-100K)

- Mereka lebih mungkin reply
- Akumulasi banyak medium followers = FR tinggi juga
- log(50K × 10 replies) = log(500000) ≈ 5.7
