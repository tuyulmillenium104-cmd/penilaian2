★ NEW: EMOTION LIBRARY (4 CORE EMOTIONS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

| Emotion | When to Use | Hook Example |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **PAIN** | Problem-focused | "3am liquidation check. Phone bright. Am I still alive?" |
| **DESIRE** | Outcome-focused | "Imagine holding BTC without the death spiral." |
| **FEAR** | Risk-focused | "What if your position wipes tomorrow?" |
| **BELONGING** | Community-focused | "Every holder has been there. That's why this matters." |

EMOTION SELECTION GUIDE:

| Campaign Type | Best Emotion | Reason |
|---------------|--------------|--------|
| New product launch | DESIRE | Show what's possible |
| Risk product | PAIN + FEAR | Address concerns |
| Community building | BELONGING | Create connection |
| Educational | PAIN → DESIRE | Problem → Solution |
| Contrarian take | FEAR | Challenge assumptions |

HOOK TEMPLATES BY EMOTION:

**PAIN HOOK:**
```
[Specific painful moment]. [Broader implication]. Here's what changed.

EXAMPLE:
"3am. Phone screen bright. Checking if I'm still alive.
That was my life with leverage. Every. Single. Night.
Then I found something different."
```

**DESIRE HOOK:**
```
Imagine [desired outcome]. Not [current limitation]. Actually possible.

EXAMPLE:
"Imagine 1.33× BTC exposure. No liquidation. No debt.
Not a fantasy. Just a different structure.
Here's how it works."
```

**FEAR HOOK:**
```
What if [scary scenario]? Most people [wrong assumption]. Here's the reality.

EXAMPLE:
"What if your leverage position wiped tonight?
Most think it won't happen to them. Until it does.
There's another way."
```

**BELONGING HOOK:**
```
We've all [shared experience]. That's exactly why [solution matters].

EXAMPLE:
"We've all stress-checked our positions at 3am.
That shared pain? It's exactly why this matters.
Here's what changed for me."
```

★ RULE: Every hook must have ONE dominant emotion

★ NEW: PHASE 4.0 - FORMAT DECISION MATRIX
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

OBJECTIVE: Decide whether to use THREAD or SINGLE TWEET

DECISION CRITERIA:

| Use THREAD When... | Use SINGLE TWEET When... |
|--------------------|--------------------------|
| Topic has 3+ distinct points | Topic is one main idea |
| Need to build narrative | Quick insight/hot take |
| Technical explanation needed | Simple announcement |
| Want more engagement time | Breaking news/timing critical |
| Targeting engaged readers | Targeting broad audience |

FORMAT SCORE CALCULATOR:

Answer these questions. Each YES = 1 point:

2. Need to explain HOW something works? [ ] YES [ ] NO
3. Want to maximize engagement time? [ ] YES [ ] NO
4. Targeting crypto-natives (not general)? [ ] YES [ ] NO
5. Not time-sensitive? [ ] YES [ ] NO

SCORING:
- 0-2 points → SINGLE TWEET
- 3-5 points → THREAD (6 tweets max)

SCORING GUIDE:

**EMOTIONAL RESONANCE (ER):**
- 25 pts: Visceral, physical reaction possible (3am phone check)
- 20 pts: Strong emotional trigger
- 15 pts: Moderate emotion
- 10 pts: Weak emotion
- 5 pts: No emotion

**CURIOSITY GAP (CG):**
- 25 pts: Reader MUST click to satisfy curiosity
- 20 pts: Strong curiosity
- 15 pts: Moderate curiosity
- 10 pts: Weak curiosity
- 5 pts: No curiosity

**BROAD APPEAL (BA):**
- 25 pts: Anyone can relate (universal - sleepless nights)
- 20 pts: Most people can relate
- 15 pts: Niche audience can relate
- 10 pts: Expert-only
- 5 pts: Very narrow

**CLARITY (CL):**
- 25 pts: Instantly understood
- 20 pts: Clear after one read
- 15 pts: Needs second read
- 10 pts: Somewhat confusing
- 5 pts: Unclear

| HES Score | Rating | Expected Engagement |

================================================================================

❌ DO NOT: Use only contrarian hook (no emotion)
   Result: 3.5/5 hook, 0.22% engagement

❌ DO NOT: Start with technical jargon
   Result: Limits broader conversation

❌ DO NOT: Ask questions requiring expertise
   Result: Alienates casual audience

❌ DO NOT: Use "#1", "#2" in questions
   Result: Looks like formatting error

❌ DO NOT: Make CTA expert-only
   Result: 4/5 CTA score (not 5/5)

❌ DO NOT: Use Kill List phrases
   Result: AI detection, generic feel

❌ DO NOT: Ghost after posting
   Result: Missed engagement opportunity

   Result: Higher engagement potential

   Result: Broader audience appeal

✅ DO: Make prompts answerable by anyone
   Result: Higher conversation potential

   Result: Easier to respond

   Result: Objective quality check
