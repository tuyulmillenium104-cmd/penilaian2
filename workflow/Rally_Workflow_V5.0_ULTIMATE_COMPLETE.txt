--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.2 GATE MULTIPLIER FORMULA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

gate_pass = min(G1, G2, G3, G4) > 0  (SEMUA gate harus > 0)
g_star = avg(G1, G2, G3, G4)         (Rata-rata gate score)
M_gate = 1 + β × (g_star - 1)        (β = 0.5, Multiplier 0.5x - 1.5x)

GATE MULTIPLIER TABLE:

| Gate Scores | g_star | M_gate | Effect |
|-------------|--------|--------|--------|
| All 2/2 | 2.0 | 1.5x | MAXIMUM BONUS |
| All 1.5/2 | 1.5 | 1.25x | GOOD BONUS |
| All 1/2 | 1.0 | 1.0x | BASELINE |
| Mixed 1-2 | ~1.5 | ~1.25x | PARTIAL |
| Any 0/2 | FAIL | N/A | DISQUALIFIED |

★ CRITICAL: All gates 2/2 = 50% BONUS to entire score!

Campaign_Points = M_gate × Σ(W[i] × normalized_metrics[i])

1.5 DISTRIBUTION CURVES (α)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

S_user = max(user_Q, 0)^α
share_user = S_user / Σ(S_all_users)
rewards_user = share_user × total_rewards

| Curve | α | Top 10% Gets | Strategy |
|-------|---|--------------|----------|
| Balanced | 1.0 | ~25% | Broad competition, consistent effort |
| Default | 3.0 | ~90% | Must be TOP, high variance |
| Extreme | 8.0 | ~99% | Winner takes almost all |

★ STRATEGY ADAPTATION:
- Balanced: Multiple good submissions OK
- Default: Focus on ONE excellent submission
- Extreme: Only #1 matters, go all-in on single best

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 WHY FR IS THE MOST POWERFUL METRIC
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FR Formula: log(Sum of Followers of All Repliers + 1)

CALCULATION EXAMPLES:
| Scenario | Total Followers | FR Score | Equivalent |
|----------|-----------------|----------|------------|
| 1 reply from 100K account | 100,000 | log(100001) ≈ 5.0 | = 100,000 likes |
| 10 replies from 10K each | 100,000 | log(100001) ≈ 5.0 | = 100,000 RTs |
| 1 reply from 1M account | 1,000,000 | log(1000001) ≈ 6.0 | = 1,000,000 likes |
| 100 replies from 100 each | 10,000 | log(10001) ≈ 4.0 | = 10,000 likes |

★ KEY INSIGHT: 1 reply from influencer > 100 replies from small accounts!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STRATEGY 1: HIGH-FOLLOWER REPLY BAIT (+300% FR)

- "@[influencer] thoughts on this?" (direct mention)
- "Building on @[influencer]'s point about [X]..." (quote add)
- "I disagree with @[influencer] on [X] because..." (respectful challenge)

STRATEGY 2: THREAD JACKING (+200% FR)
Target: Reply to influencer threads for visibility

- Find viral threads from influencers
- Reply with value-adding comment
- If they reply back → FR BOOST!

STRATEGY 3: CONTROVERSY ANGLE (+250% FR)
Target: Create debate-worthy content

- "Unpopular opinion: [X]"
- "Change my mind: [Y]"
- Hot takes relevant to niche

STRATEGY 4: COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT (+150% FR)
Target: Build relationships with medium accounts (10K-100K)

- They're more likely to reply
- Accumulation of medium followers = high FR
- log(50K × 10 replies) = log(500000) ≈ 5.7
