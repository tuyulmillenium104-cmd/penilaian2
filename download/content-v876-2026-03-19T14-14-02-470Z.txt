Smart contracts execute code with mathematical perfection, but have you ever stopped to wonder what happens when that cold logic collides with the messy nuance of human intent? It is a fascinating paradox—almost a ticking time bomb. When the inevitable gap between rigid code and fluid agreement appears, the entire transaction freezes. It really makes you ask: why have we allowed our digital infrastructure to remain shackled by these archaic physical world limitations? We built an economy that moves at the speed of light, yet our dispute resolution remains frustratingly earthbound. Relying on centralized courts to enforce borderless digital agreements creates a jurisdictional nightmare that forces us to wonder: is there a better way? This is the very mystery Internet Court was designed to solve. It introduces a decentralized framework that sparks a new era of rapid, transparent enforcement operating natively with digital assets and DAOs. It makes you curious about the full potential we are finally unlocking by moving dispute resolution onchain.
