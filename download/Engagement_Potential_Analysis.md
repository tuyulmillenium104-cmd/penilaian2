## 📋 ENGAGEMENT POTENTIAL CHECKLIST (NEW!)

### Hook Checklist (Tweet 1):

- [ ] **Accessible**: Can non-experts understand the hook?
- [ ] **Relatable**: Is there a scenario anyone can visualize?
- [ ] **Curiosity gap**: Does it make people want to read more?
- [ ] **Contrarian/Bold**: Is there a statement that challenges assumptions?

### Content Flow Checklist (Tweets 2-5):

- [ ] **Progressive complexity**: Starts accessible, builds to technical
- [ ] **Analogies**: Used for technical concepts?
- [ ] **Broader context**: Connected to bigger trends?
- [ ] **Value for all**: Expert gets depth, casual reader gets insight

### CTA Checklist (Final Tweet):

- [ ] **Easy to respond**: Doesn't require expert knowledge?
- [ ] **Opinion-eligible**: Anyone can have an opinion?
- [ ] **Specific enough**: Not vague?
- [ ] **Natural fit**: Doesn't feel forced?
