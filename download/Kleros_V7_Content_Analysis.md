## Knowledge Base Summary

**What is Internet Court?**
- Decentralized dispute resolution framework for digital conflicts
- Internet-native enforcement layer
- Faster, borderless, compatible with digital assets and smart contracts

**Core Problem:**
- Traditional courts are geographically bound
- Legal processes are slow and expensive
- Cross-border enforcement is complex
- Smart contracts execute automatically even when disputes arise

**Why This Matters in Web3:**
- Smart contracts remove intermediaries but don't remove disputes
- Bugs, misaligned incentives, governance conflicts, oracle failures, fraud
- No clear, efficient way to resolve disputes without centralized actors

**Why Now:**
- Growth of DAOs, DeFi, NFT markets
- AI-driven agents increasing probability of digital disputes
- Economic coordination becoming more automated and borderless
